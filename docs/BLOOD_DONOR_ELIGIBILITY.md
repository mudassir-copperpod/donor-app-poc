# Blood Donor Eligibility Requirements

This document outlines the eligibility requirements for all supported animal species in the blood donation program, based on veterinary industry standards.

## Supported Species

The application now supports **11 different species** that can donate blood:

### 1. Dogs
- **Age Range**: 1-8 years
- **Minimum Weight**: 55 lbs (Eligible), 45 lbs (Temporarily Ineligible)
- **Blood Types**: DEA 1.1 Positive, DEA 1.1 Negative, Universal Donor
- **Notes**: Most common blood donors. Universal donors (DEA 1.1 negative) are highly valuable.

### 2. Cats
- **Age Range**: 1-8 years
- **Minimum Weight**: 10 lbs (Eligible), 8 lbs (Temporarily Ineligible)
- **Blood Types**: Type A, Type B, Type AB
- **Notes**: Type A is most common. Type B cats should only receive Type B blood.

### 3. Horses
- **Age Range**: 2-20 years
- **Minimum Weight**: 900 lbs
- **Blood Types**: AA Positive, QA Positive
- **Notes**: Large volume donors. Require specialized facilities.

### 4. Rabbits
- **Age Range**: 1-6 years
- **Minimum Weight**: 9 lbs
- **Blood Types**: Universal
- **Notes**: Used primarily for exotic pet transfusions.

### 5. Ferrets
- **Age Range**: 1-5 years
- **Minimum Weight**: 2 lbs
- **Blood Types**: Universal
- **Notes**: Small volume donors. Used for other ferrets and exotic pets.

### 6. Goats
- **Age Range**: 1-8 years
- **Minimum Weight**: 60 lbs
- **Blood Types**: Type A, Type B
- **Notes**: Common in rural veterinary medicine.

### 7. Sheep
- **Age Range**: 1-7 years
- **Minimum Weight**: 80 lbs
- **Blood Types**: Type A, Type B
- **Notes**: Similar to goats in donation protocols.

### 8. Pigs
- **Age Range**: 1-5 years
- **Minimum Weight**: 150 lbs
- **Blood Types**: Universal
- **Notes**: Increasingly used in veterinary medicine.

### 9. Cows
- **Age Range**: 2-10 years
- **Minimum Weight**: 800 lbs
- **Blood Types**: Type A, Type B, Type AB
- **Notes**: Large volume donors for livestock medicine.

### 10. Llamas
- **Age Range**: 2-15 years
- **Minimum Weight**: 250 lbs
- **Blood Types**: Camelid Universal
- **Notes**: Can donate to both llamas and alpacas.

### 11. Alpacas
- **Age Range**: 2-15 years
- **Minimum Weight**: 250 lbs
- **Blood Types**: Camelid Universal
- **Notes**: Can donate to both alpacas and llamas.

## General Eligibility Criteria

All donor animals must meet the following general requirements:

### Health Requirements
- Good overall health
- Up-to-date on vaccinations
- Free from infectious diseases
- No history of blood-borne diseases
- Never received a blood transfusion
- Not currently on medications (with some exceptions)

### Behavioral Requirements
- Calm temperament
- Cooperative during procedures
- No aggressive behavior

### Lifestyle Requirements
- Regular veterinary care
- Proper nutrition
- Not pregnant or nursing
- Not actively breeding

## Donation Frequency

- **Dogs**: Every 8-12 weeks
- **Cats**: Every 8-12 weeks
- **Horses**: Every 8-12 weeks
- **Other Species**: Varies by species and veterinary guidance

## Blood Volume Collection

Collection volumes are species-specific and based on body weight:

- **Dogs**: 10-20 ml/kg (450-500 ml typical)
- **Cats**: 10-15 ml/kg (50-60 ml typical)
- **Horses**: 15-20% of blood volume (6-8 liters typical)
- **Small Animals**: Proportional to body weight

## Disqualifying Factors

### Permanent Disqualifications
- History of blood-borne diseases
- Previous blood transfusion recipient
- Chronic health conditions
- Age outside acceptable range

### Temporary Disqualifications
- Recent illness or surgery
- Current medications
- Pregnancy or nursing
- Recent vaccination (within 2 weeks)
- Weight below minimum threshold
- Recent travel to endemic disease areas

## Implementation Notes

The application automatically evaluates eligibility based on:
1. Species-specific age requirements
2. Species-specific weight requirements
3. Health questionnaire responses
4. Veterinary records

Animals that don't meet all criteria are marked as:
- **Eligible**: Ready to donate
- **Temporarily Ineligible**: May become eligible with time/weight gain
- **Ineligible**: Does not meet basic requirements
- **Pending Review**: Requires veterinary evaluation
