## Quick Add Instructions

1. **Login** to the app (or register a new account)
2. **Navigate** to the Pets tab
3. **Click** the + (Add) button
4. **Fill in** the form using any of the profiles above
5. **Submit** to add the pet

## Eligibility Criteria (for reference)

**Dogs:**
- Minimum weight: 55 lbs
- Age: 1-8 years old
- Must be healthy and up-to-date on vaccinations

**Cats:**
- Minimum weight: 10 lbs
- Age: 1-8 years old
- Indoor only preferred

**Horses:**
- Minimum weight: 900 lbs
- Age: 2-20 years old
- Must be healthy and well-tempered
